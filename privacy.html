DayReset Privacy

English

DayReset stores routine names, notes, reminder times, and daily completion checks on your device.

DayReset does not require an account, does not include ads, and does not use analytics or third-party tracking SDKs in this release.

Notification permission is optional and is used only for local routine reminders.

简体中文

DayReset 会在你的设备上保存例行事项名称、备注、提醒时间和每日完成记录。

此版本不需要账号，不包含广告，也不使用分析或第三方跟踪 SDK。

通知权限是可选的，仅用于本地例行事项提醒。
